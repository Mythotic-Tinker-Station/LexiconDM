 Creation
-ออออออออ-
They were created for the Eternal Deathmatch project, an excellent ninteen
level add-on deathmatch pack for id Software's Doom II. Eternal Deathmatch
(EDM.ZIP) can be found in the Action Games Forum on Compuserve (GO ACTION)
and on the Internet at ftp.cdrom.com/pub/idgames/themes/TeamTNT.

For more information on distribution rights, read the "EDM.TXT" file that
is included with Eternal Deathmatch.

 The Tools
-อออออออออ-
Both authors composed on an AWE-32 with a 8mb EMU Technologies soundbank
loaded into 8mb of on-board AWE-32 ram, and Twelve Tone Systems' Cakewalk
Professional v3.01.

Rich used a Casio CZ-1 keyboard (yeah I know it's old, damn thing weighs
a ton too <g>).

Adam used his poor mouse that will never be the same again. :(
