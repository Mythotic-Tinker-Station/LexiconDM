// Due to how the Lexicon Wad Extractor works, the doors and switches
// will have to have their sound sequences defined individually for each
// wad that the tool extracts.

	0 DSDOROPN
	1 DSDORCLS
	2 DSBDOPN
	3 DSBDCLS
]

	stopsound plats/pt1_stop
end

	stopsound plats/pt1_stop
end

:DW20Ceiling
